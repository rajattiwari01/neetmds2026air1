🌅 Daily Routine Template (10 Hours)

  • 6:00 AM - 6:30 AM: Wake up, freshen up, quick review of yesterday's notes
  • 6:30 AM - 10:30 AM: 📖 Deep Study Session 1 (New topic - 4 hours)
  • 10:30 AM - 11:00 AM: Break + light snack
  • 11:00 AM - 2:00 PM: 📝 MCQ Practice + PYQ Solving (3 hours)
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Lunch + Power nap (20 min max)
  • 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM: 🔄 Revision of previous day's topics (2 hours)
  • 5:00 PM - 5:30 PM: Break + light exercise/walk
  • 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM: 🖼️ Image-based Qs + Mnemonics (1 hour)
  • 7:00 PM onwards: Dinner, relax, review tomorrow's plan
  • Before sleep: Read classification/table summaries for 15 mins

💡 High-Yield Topics (Repeat in PYQs)

  • 🔴 Oral Path: Cysts, Tumors, Premalignant lesions, Salivary gland tumors, Odontogenic tumors
  • 🔴 Oral Med: Vesiculobullous lesions, White lesions, TMJ disorders, Radiographic interpretations
  • 🔴 Oral Surgery: LA techniques, Impactions, Fractures, Suturing, Implants, Cysts
  • 🔴 Prostho: RPD components, Impression materials, Occlusion, Implant prosthodontics, Denture problems
  • 🔴 Conservative: Cavity classifications, Composites, Glass Ionomer, Pulp capping, Bleaching
  • 🔴 Endo: Root canal irrigants, Obturation, Working length, Instruments, Perforations
  • 🔴 Ortho: Cephalometrics (ALL analyses), Growth & Development, Wire properties, Appliances
  • 🔴 Perio: Classifications (new & old), Surgical techniques, Host modulation, Regeneration
  • 🔴 Pedo: Behavior management, Space maintainers, Traumatic injuries, Fluoride
  • 🔴 Dental Materials: Setting reactions, Properties comparison, Impression materials, Cements

⚡ Power Tips for NEET MDS

  • ✅ Solve at least 100 MCQs daily from Day 1
  • ✅ Mark & revise wrong answers in a separate notebook
  • ✅ Make tables & comparisons for similar topics
  • ✅ Focus on image-based questions — increasing every year
  • ✅ Classifications are must-know — make a master sheet
  • ✅ Recent advances — Check last 2-3 years' new topics
  • ✅ Drug doses — Top 30 dental drugs must be memorized
  • ✅ Mnemonics — Create your own for complex topics
  • ✅ Sunday = Mock test + Analysis (from Phase 3 onwards)
  • ✅ Never skip revision — It's more important than new learning

🧠 Mental Health & Exam Tips

  • 🧘 Take 1 half-day off per week — sustainability > burnout
  • 💤 Sleep 7-8 hours — memory consolidation happens during sleep
  • 🏃 Exercise 30 min daily — even a walk helps
  • 🍎 Eat well — brain needs nutrition to function
  • 📱 Social media detox — limit to 30 min/day max
  • 👥 Find a study buddy for accountability
  • 📊 Track progress — use this planner daily!
  • 🎯 Exam day: Read instructions, manage time (1 min/MCQ), don't change answers
  • 💪 Remember: Consistency beats intensity
